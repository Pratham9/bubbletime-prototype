Lovers of the sweet Taiwanese beverage known as bubble tea will be happy to hear there's a happy place in Kamloops to get their fix.


For those who have never tried bubble tea, it's a tea-based drink (usually served cold) that is typically mixed with toppings: milk, fruit syrup, pudding, mango stars and rainbow jelly, to name a few. What makes the tea "bubble" are the tapioca balls that are added in.

"We brew our teas fresh, per order," says Vo. "It's not batch-brewed, like the night before or the morning of, where it sits there for a long time and it goes bad."
